About Me

Hi, im Gracjan!

I am a recent Computer Science graduate with a strong passion for web development, specializing in full-stack technologies. I am proficient in JavaScript, TypeScript, Python, HTML, CSS, ReactJS, and Next.js, and I have hands-on experience building scalable, user-friendly web applications. During my internship as a Full Stack Developer at Schooly, I led the development of the Schooly.help site, managed databases using Google Cloud, and worked on implementing an AI-driven service, gaining valuable experience in both frontend and backend development.

I enjoy working on personal projects to expand my skills, such as creating a gym workout planner with Next.js and Prisma, a social media web app using the MERN stack, and a Trello clone with Supabase. These projects have allowed me to experiment with modern tools like GraphQL, server-side rendering, and real-time messaging. I also have experience with version control, project management, and agile methodologies, which help me deliver efficient and maintainable code.

I am always eager to learn new technologies and contribute to innovative web development projects. My goal is to continue growing as a developer while creating impactful and scalable web solutions.
